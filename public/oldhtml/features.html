INFRASTRUCTURE AND ITS UTILISATION :



1) Classrooms :- The college has spacious two storied RCC building. The classrooms are airy with ample natural sun light. Each classroom has fans and tube lights.

2) Library :- It is enriched with books over 10,000; periodical over 17; daily news papers over 13. Library is with daily book issuing counter and reading room. In near future, the library will be equipped with e-issuing counter computers. At present Seven PCs in library providing free internet axis to students.

3) Laboratories :- Science faculty has well maintained and well equipped laboratories. The chemistry laboratory is huge and up to the mark of standards. It is-the asset to college academics.

4) Play ground :- The play ground is with ample play area, It has cricket pitch at center. It is surrounded by lush green grass and young plants of palm. It will be facilitate by well equipped Gymkhana. At present chess, Carom, table tennis are indoor facilities for students. With over rupees four lakhs worth Gymnasium equipments.

5) Canteen :- Hygienic, delicious snacks providing canteen is at one corner of the campus.

6) Garden :- College has lush green grassed lawn. It is well maintained by sprinkler Irrigation. There are many cacti, succulents in the garden. The potted plants add in aesthetic value of college.

7) Health Care center :- College provides free medical check up, blood group check up, first aid facilities to students.

8) Multipurpose Hall :- It has over 250 sitting capacity Hall. It is airy with ample windows and fans. The decoraive backdrop, dice, podium are added advantage. It has LCD projector as added advantage. It has LCD projector as advanced teaching aids for students. Built in sound and light system, POP designed ceilling make elligant look of multipurpose hall. It is physical structure where students really enjoys mental treat of different functions, programmes.

9) Conference Hall :- Red Carpet Conference Hall is 'ultimate' feature of College, Well organised furniture, designed roof with mild light system, antichamber, LCD projector for presentation, Air conditioned system are features of conference Hall. It is for about 50 delegates. Students Council meetings, Conferences, Staff Common meetings, Management meetings etc make the maximum utility of the venue.

10) Student Corner :- It is natural venue for students. Inside the college campus students can enjoy natural joy. It is 'SPACE' for students. The place is for dine, chit-chat, write the applications, to wait for friends, share the feelings, help to each other to build bond of friendship. It has lawn, natural roof, Drinking water facilities with water purifier, ample sitting arrangement with Notice Boards to keep update informed.

11) Gymkhana :- Gymnasium - Carrom board, Chess boards, Table tennis, Volley ball, badminton, physical exercise equipment are well maintained. Bat, balls, stumps, net and mat are for student to play cricket. On play ground, special grounds are maintained for kho-kho, kabbadi, volley ball, cricket pitch facilities are available.

12) Common Room :- Boy's and Girl's Common room are present inside the College buildings. It is attached with wash room. Clean and well ventilisers keeps the area hygenic. Ample water and big mirror keeps students up to date and fresh.

13) Reading Room :- Separate reading room is there at Terace. It is of about 150 student's capacity. Cool breeze of nature with natural light enhances environment for study. Reading room provides place to learn for students. It is added advantage to students for working hard to gain more in examinations.
