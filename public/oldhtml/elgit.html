ELIGIBILTY CRITERIA FOR I.T. FACULTY

F.Y.B.Sc.(I.T.) (Sem I) H.S.C. in Science or equivalent passed (45 % marks for open & 40 % for rescued Category) at one and same sitting with maths.
OR

Diploma in any engineering branches with two years or three years Duration after the S.S.C. conducted by competent authority.
F.Y.B.Sc.(I.T.) (Sem II) A learner shall be allowed to keep term for semester II irrespective of number of grades obtained in semester I
S.Y.B.Sc. (I.T.) (Sem III) A learner shall be allowed to keep term for semester Ill-if he/she passes (grade ‘E’ or above in each course) each of Semester I & Semester II.
OR

He/she fails in not more than three courses with not more than total 200 marks, in each of semester I and semester II.(For all Science programmes, carrying less than total 900marks).
S.Y.B.Sc.(I.T.) (Sem IV) A learner shall be allowed to keep term for semester IV irrespective of grades obtained in each course of semester III However, learner has to pass either of semester I or semester II in order to appear for semester IV.
T.Y.B.Sc.(I.T.) (Sem V) A learner shall be allowed to keep term for semester V-if he/she passes each of Semester I, Semester II, semester III & Semester IV.
OR
He/she has passed Semester I and Semester II and fails in not more three courses with not more than total 200 marks, in each of semester III and semester IV.(For all Science programmes, carrying less than total 900 marks). A learner shall be allowed to keep terms for semester VI irrespective of grades obtained in each course of semester V.
T.Y.B.Sc.(I.T.) (Sem VI) A learner shall be allowed to keep term for semester VI irrespective of number of grades obtained in the semester V.
M.Sc.(I.T.) - I Passed in B.Sc.I.T faculty 45% marks/B.Sc. (CS) with 45% marks/BE with 45% marks.
M.Sc.(I.T.) - II i) A learner shall be allowed to keep term for semester II irrespective of number of heads of failure in the semester I.
ii) A learner shall be allowed to take admission and to keep terms and appear for semester III irrespective of number of heads of failure in the semester I and II.
iii) A learner after attending semester III is allowed to go to semester IV.
iv) A learner shall be allowed to keep term and allowed to appear for semester. IV irrespective of number of heads of failure in semester I, II and III, However the result of the semester IV shall be kept at abeyance until the learner passes each of the semester I, semester II, semester III.
v) A learner will have to earn a minimum of 96 credits to pass the M.Sc. degree.
vi) Those who fail in any course in any semester will have to clear the same in the examination conducted in the corresponding odd or even semester.
